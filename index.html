About Us

Founded: 28th June 2019 by Mr. Satyam Pandey

SWAYAM ENERGY is a grassroots NGO working to uplift individuals through education, skill development, employment, and social welfare. With strong leadership and support from volunteers and social workers, we aim to create a confident and self-reliant India.

Vision: To build a confident, self-reliant society through inclusive education, employment, and social development.

Mission: To empower communities by providing access to education, skills, and sustainable livelihoods, enabling positive social change.

Our Work

  • Education: Free tuition classes, digital literacy, school supply drives
  • Employment & Skill Development: Vocational training, computer courses, placement support
  • Social Welfare: Health and hygiene awareness, clean water campaigns
  • Youth Empowerment: Leadership workshops, personal development sessions
